About

My research is focused on statistical learning theory and machine learning. In particular, I work on information-theoretic and PAC-Bayesian generalization bounds, and the application of these bounds to neural networks and meta learning, as well as online learning.

I am currently at Chalmers University of Technology, where I recently completed my PhD in the Communication Systems group in the Department of Electrical Engineering, supervised by Giuseppe Durisi, as an affiliated PhD student within the WASP Graduate School AI-track. Prior to this, I studied Physics at the University of Gothenburg.
